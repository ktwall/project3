Fall Gymnastics Class Schedule 2021

Gardens Gymnastics will be closed for all Federal Holidays. Please view our Facebook page for the most up to date calendar.

Parent / Child Gymnastics

This exciting class is filled with singing, jumping, climbing and tumbling. Class time includes circle time, individualized gymnastics attention, parachute play and bubble time. It is the perfect start to help build your toddlers physical, cognitive and social skills in a positive learning environment.

Preschool Gymnastics

This independent class includes non-stop gymnastics fun. The fundamentals of gymnastics are developed as children improve their skills on various gymnastics equipment. This class teaches self confidence, coordination and balance.

Recreational Gymnastics

This class uses a safe and encouraging method to teach gymnastics in a positive atmosphere. Our 12-week session will include strength training, flexibility and skill progressions, all of which will help build a healthy mind and body.

To be prepared for class: wear a leotard or athletic clothing, tie up long hair and leave all jewelry and accessories at home.

Accepted payment: Checks made out to Gardens Gymnastics, Cash or Zelle (561) 315-6338.

All classes are co-ed.

Location: 1200 Prosperity Farms Road, North Palm Beach, Florida
